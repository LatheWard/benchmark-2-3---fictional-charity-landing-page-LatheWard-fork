Kicks for Kids

Welcome!

Kicks for kids is an anonymous, nonprofit organization that allows you to simply drop off a pair of shoes for a child in need.

How we started

In 1996, our founder Mike Stokes was homeless, broken, and had one good pair of shoes to his name. During this time span of his life, he was fortunate enough to get a winning lottery ticket of $300,000, invested his money, and ever since has dedicated his life to helping children.
"I grew up without much, but that time in my life was pretty bad for me. I want to give back to this community that has helped me so much."
Mike Stokes
In the year 2010, Kicks for Kids was born. With HQ right here in Pontotoc, MS, there is so much we have been able to accomplish for the community.

How you can donate (It's so easy, everyone can do it)

All you have to do is drop of a good pair of shoes at our facility in the drop box. See the front desk for any other types of donations.
